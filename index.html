Border Collies are awesome

The Border Collie is a British breed of herding dog of medium size. It originates in the region of the Anglo-Scottish border, and descends from the traditional sheepdogs once found all over the British Isles.

Border Collie Information

Collies are medium-sized dogs with a moderate amount of coat.

It has been claimed that it is the "most intelligent" dog.

Collies require daily physical exercise and mental stimulation.

The natural life span of the collie is between 12 and 15 years.
